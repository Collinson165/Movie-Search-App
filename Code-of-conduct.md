# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a friendly and respectful environment for everyone involved in our project. We welcome participants of all backgrounds and beliefs, and we value respectful and constructive interactions.

## Expected Behavior

We ask that everyone participating in our project:

- Uses welcoming and inclusive language.
- Treats others with respect and kindness.
- Listens to different viewpoints and provides constructive feedback.
- Focuses on the well-being of the community.
- Shows empathy and understanding toward others.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Using inappropriate language or sharing explicit content.
- Engaging in personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments.
- Harassing others, either publicly or privately.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Any behavior that could be considered inappropriate in a professional context.

## Responsibility

Project maintainers are responsible for ensuring that the standards of behavior are upheld. They may take appropriate action, including editing or removing content, or restricting participation, if behavior violates this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported by contacting the [project team](collinsoga2@gmail.com). All reports will be reviewed and treated with confidentiality. The project team will respond in a manner deemed necessary and appropriate.
